## Part 1: Art Clokey and Gumby — The Independent Creator

### Who Art Clokey Was
- Created Gumby in the 1950s — one of the most recognized characters in American culture
- Pioneer of stop-motion clay animation
- Refused merchandising for 8 years: "I was a very idealistic person, and I didn't want to exploit children"
- Called Gumby "an act of love to children"
- Spiritual seeker — seminary student, Buddhist, follower of Sai Baba. Documentary about him titled *Gumby Dharma*
- Ran his own independent studio (Premavision) — never network-owned
- Also created *Davey and Goliath*, a Christian educational series for the Lutheran Church
- Trained more than half the animators who worked on Tim Burton's *Nightmare Before Christmas* — his people went to Pixar and Disney, but he stayed independent
- Died January 8, 2010, at age 88

### Gumby's Philosophy
- Clay doesn't command; it becomes. It transforms.
- Gumby moved through his world not as a rigid hero but as a living metaphor
- Green — the color of life and growth — was deliberate
- His form resembles a sprouting seedling: legs like roots, arms like branches, head leaning toward the sun
- Gumby entered stories through the portals of books — imagination as a doorway into other worlds
- A quiet argument for freedom through creativity, not control

## Part 5: Methods — Induced Brain Bleeds

### Declassified CIA Assassination Methods (Church Committee, 1975)

**The "Heart Attack Gun":**

**The Umbrella Gun:**

**Project MKNaomi:**
- CIA and Army program to develop biological agents

### Methods That Could Induce a Brain Bleed

**Anticoagulant poisoning:**
- Warfarin or superwarfarins (brodifacoum, bromadiolone — rodent poisons)
- Increase risk of intracerebral hemorrhage 5-10x
- Mortality from anticoagulant-induced brain bleed exceeds 50%
- Superwarfarins are tasteless and colorless — can be added to food or drink
- Blood levels not readily detectable in standard tests
- A standard autopsy finding "cerebral hemorrhage" would likely not investigate the cause of the hemorrhage
- Only detectable if specifically tested for via liquid chromatography mass spectrometry (LC-MS/MS)

**Blood pressure manipulation:**
- Substances that spike blood pressure can rupture weakened blood vessels
- Sympathomimetic drugs, high-dose stimulants, hypertensive crisis inducers
- Autopsy finds "brain bleed caused by hypertension" and stops

### What Would Show Evidence

**What would have shown it at the time (2018):**
- Abnormally elevated PT/INR (clotting times) in a man not on blood thinners
- Depleted vitamin K-dependent clotting factors (II, VII, IX, X)
- Direct serum testing for superwarfarins via LC-MS/MS
- These tests are only run if someone suspects poisoning

**What could still be detected now (if buried, not cremated):**
- Exhumation toxicology can detect substances years after burial
- Opioids: 100% detection rate after 9+ years
- Benzodiazepines: 60% after 9+ years
- Superwarfarins have extremely long half-lives (20-130 days) and bind tightly to tissue
- Best tissues: muscle, liver, bone marrow, hair
