## The HOW — Technology

Without the technology, the media is just philosophy. Beautiful. True. But unable to give people something to do. The technology makes the philosophy livable — literally.

### adaptive_Automation/

The Adaptive Genome Design System — the instrument the Spiral Steward wields. A tool for designing novel biological sequences by combining functional elements from across the tree of life.

**North star:** Grow living houses — organisms that are structurally functional, self-repairing, and environmentally adaptive.

- Retrieval layer: UCSC Genome Browser (220+ genomes) + NCBI Entrez
- Compatibility engine: codon optimization, regulatory analysis, pathway conflict detection
- Assembly engine: adaptive sequence construction with conflict resolution
- Output: FASTA sequences, pathway maps, AlphaFold handoff packages
