## LinkedIn Post

Two hundred ants live in a jar. Half are red, half are black. They coexist. They share space. They do not fight.

Then someone shakes the jar.

Now the red ants see black ants biting them. The black ants see red ants attacking them. They go to war — because the shaking made proximity feel like aggression.

Neither side looks up. Neither side asks: who shook the jar?

This is the oldest trick in the history of power. It works on ants. It works on nations. It works on you, right now, every time your feed fills with rage at the other side.

The demagogue does not create the differences. The differences are real. What the demagogue does is shake the jar — and then hand you a magnifying glass pointed at your neighbor instead of upward.
