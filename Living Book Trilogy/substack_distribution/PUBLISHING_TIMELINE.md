- Your master's research directly feeds Book III — new experiments, new data, new figures.
- Your Substack audience follows the research journey in real time. They're invested before Book III drops.
- Faculty advisors see a student who publishes and communicates science to a public audience — that's an asset, not a distraction.

By this point, readers have spent 7 months with your philosophy, your science, and your family's story told through admiration. When the cartel chapter arrives, they have context. They trust you. It lands differently than if you led with it.

## THE PRINCIPLE

You are not marketing a product. You are planting seeds.

Every Substack post is a seed. Every LinkedIn comment is water. Every podcast appearance is sunlight. Every conference talk is a graft. Every thesis experiment is root growth. The book is the tree. You do not ship trees. You grow them.

The audience you build before launch IS the launch. The serialization IS the marketing. The philosophy IS the brand. The master's IS the proof.

Two tracks, one spiral. The research and the writing are not separate careers — they are the same work expressed in two languages. One for journals. One for everyone else. That is the Spiral Steward.

The first job was gardener, not king. This timeline is a garden plan.
