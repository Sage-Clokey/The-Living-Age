The Living Age

Life builds without blueprints or bosses — we're building the technology to design with that principle.

The Problem

We build civilization from dead materials using top-down engineering. Buildings don't grow, heal, or adapt. Every structure begins deteriorating the moment it's finished.

The Insight

Biology already solved this. Gene regulatory networks self-organize without central control. Forests, coral reefs, mycelium networks — life builds through distributed intelligence.

The Technology

The Adaptive Genome Design System — a pipeline that retrieves genes from 220+ genomes, analyzes cross-species compatibility, and assembles novel biological sequences. Currently working. North star: organisms that grow into architecture.
